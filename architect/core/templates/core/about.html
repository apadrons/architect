About

Welcome to the online home of Joseph M Hyland, an architect based in South River, NJ, serving the architectural needs of Central Jersey and beyond...

Our Story

We bring a unique blend of passion, creativity, and expertise to every project. With a deep-rooted commitment to innovation and sustainability, we strive to push the boundaries of traditional architecture, creating spaces that inspire and endure.

Expertise

With years of experience in the field, We have successfully completed a diverse range of projects, spanning residential, commercial, institutional, and cultural sectors. From sleek modern designs to historic preservation, our portfolio showcases our dedication to excellence and attention to detail.

Teaching Experience

In addition to our professional practice, We are proud to have served as a teacher at Rutgers University, sharing our knowledge and insights with the next generation of architects. This experience has not only enriched our understanding of the field but also reinforced our commitment to education and mentorship.

Commitment to Community

As a local architect, We are deeply invested in the communities we serve. We believe in the transformative power of architecture to enhance the quality of life and foster connections within neighborhoods. Whether revitalizing urban spaces or designing sustainable homes, we approach each project with a deep sense of responsibility and dedication to making a positive impact.

Get in Touch

Explore our portfolio to discover our past projects and envision the possibilities for your next venture. We welcome collaborations with clients who share our vision for bold, innovative design. Contact us today to discuss how we can bring your architectural dreams to life.
